What is Anime?

Anime is a style of animation that originated in Japan. It is known for its colorful artwork, unique characters, and emotional storytelling.

Popular anime includes shows like Naruto, One Piece, and Attack on Titan.

Popular Genres

Action

Fighting, adventure, and intense battles.

Romance

Love stories and emotional connections.

Fantasy

Magic, supernatural worlds, and imagination.

Isekai

Characters transported to another world.
